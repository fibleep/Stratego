LEAD YOUR MEN TO VICTORY

Deploy your troops and make history


With 40 pieces in hand you must outwit your opponent, your Marshal is your strongest unit, but you must protect him from the Spy.
Your General, Colonels, Majors, Captains, Lieutenants, Sergeants will give their life for you.

If you know the enemy and know yourself, you need not fear the result of a hundred battles
Sun Tzu

Information is key, the Scouts regiment will help you with that, use them wisely. With evolution of warfare, Bombs have appeared on the battlefield - use Miners to defuse them.
How many lives will you sacrifice to reach the Flag?

THE MARSHAL

Amount: 1
Abilities: NONE
Rank: X

The Marshal is your most capable unit, throughout his service, he rose to the highest rank in the military and has now sworn loyalty to you.
The Marshal will win against any opposing unit, he has one vulnerability - The Spy.
If The Spy initiates combat, The Marshal is captured.

THE SPY

Amount: 1
Abilities: Capture The Marshal
Rank: S

The Spy hides in the dark, waiting to strike. While he's the weakest piece on the board, he's capable of capturing the opposing Marshal.
Watch out though, if The Marshal attacks first, The Spy is removed.
